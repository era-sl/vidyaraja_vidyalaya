         Sinhala Literary Association




                                                  
  • Grade 6 to 11 all students should participate in Sinhala Literary Association.


  • The Sinhala Literary Association is held on every month 1st week friday from 12.00 pm to 1.30 pm.


  • Sinhala drama, songs, dance, speeches, and special events are presented by Sinhala Literary Association's students.



         English Literary Association




                                                  
  • Grade 6 to 11 all students should participate in English Literary Association


  • English Literary Association is held on every month 2nd week Friday from 12.00 pm to 1.30 pm.


  • English drama, songs, dance, speeches, and special events are presented by English Literary Association's students.



         Dancing Societies

         Music Societies

         ICT Societies
